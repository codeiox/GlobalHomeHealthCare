Privacy Policy

At Global Home Health Care, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and submit applications or inquiries.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information you provide to us when filling out forms on our website, including but not limited to:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Home address
  • Apartment number
  • City, State, ZIP code

  • Messages or descriptions included in the application form

How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used for the following purposes:

  • To process and respond to your job application
  • To communicate with you regarding your submission
  • To maintain our internal records and comply with legal obligations
  • To improve our website and services

Consent

By using our site and submitting your information, you consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.

Protection of Information

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Uploaded resumes and form data are stored securely in our internal systems and are only accessible by authorized personnel.

Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze website traffic. By continuing to use our site, you accept our use of cookies. You may choose to disable cookies through your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

We utilize Google reCAPTCHA to secure our forms against spam. Your use of reCAPTCHA is subject to Google's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected, and to comply with applicable legal, regulatory, or policy requirements.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share data with trusted third parties only when necessary for the operation of our services or as required by law.

Your Rights

You may request to access, correct, or delete your personal data by contacting us. We will comply with such requests in accordance with applicable laws.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date below. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

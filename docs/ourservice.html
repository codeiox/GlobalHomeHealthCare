Combining experience and professionalism in providing care.

At Global Home Health Care, our range of services is designed to improve the quality of life for our clients while promoting independence and well-being. We approach each care plan with personalized attention and clinical excellence.

Core Services

  • Skilled Nursing: Professional care such as wound care, injections, post-operative recovery, and chronic illness support.
  • Home Health Aides: Daily personal care including bathing, dressing, and grooming assistance.
  • Physical Therapy: Restoration of movement and strength through customized therapeutic exercises.
  • Occupational Therapy: Assistance with regaining independence in day-to-day activities like dressing, cooking, and mobility.
  • Medical Social Work: Connecting patients to vital community resources, counseling, and long-term care planning.
  • 24-Hour On-Call Nurse: Around-the-clock availability for emergency care or medical questions.
